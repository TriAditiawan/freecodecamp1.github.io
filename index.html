Career

"His fame was not only catapulted by his profession as a film artist, but also thanks to his appearance in the entertainment world in Indonesia. Apart from frequently appearing in various magic shows and talk shows about magic on various television stations, he has also starred in a number of advertisements on television and print media. Deddy also had time to play in a number of soap operas as a guest star. His fame is mainly supported by his skill in mesmerizing the audience who witnessed the action. In addition to general magic tricks, Deddy also often presents magic shows that contain high risks. Despite being known as a magician, he himself prefers to be referred to as a "mentalist"."
